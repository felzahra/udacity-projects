SECURE PASSWORD RULES
=====================

Rule 1: Minimum Length
- Password must be at least 12 characters long
- Longer passwords (16+ characters) are strongly recommended

Rule 2: Character Diversity
Password must contain at least:
- 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
- 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
- 1 digit (0-9)
- 1 special character (!@#$%^&*()_+-=[]{}|;:,.<>?)

Rule 3: Avoid Common Patterns
- No sequential characters (abcd, 1234)
- No repeated characters (aaa, 111)
- No keyboard patterns (qwerty, asdfgh)
- No personal information (names, birth dates, etc.)

EXAMPLE PASSWORDS
=================

Example 1: G7k#9mP$x2@L
- Length: 12 characters
- Contains uppercase: G, P, L
- Contains lowercase: k, m, x
- Contains digits: 7, 9, 2
- Contains special: #, $, @
- No obvious patterns or sequences

Example 2: J8p$3nR!w5qT&b2X
- Length: 16 characters
- Multiple uppercase and lowercase letters
- Multiple digits and special characters
- Complex and unpredictable pattern
- Very strong against brute force attacks
